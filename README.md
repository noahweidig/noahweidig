## 🛰️ Geospatial & Data Science Focus

- **Wildfire risk & WUI exposure modeling** that blends burn probability, suppression capacity, and socio-environmental vulnerability metrics.
- **Remote sensing pipelines** for monitoring fuel conditions, burn severity, and vegetation dynamics using Landsat, Sentinel, GEDI, and NAIP imagery.
- **Open-source spatial analytics** with reproducible workflows powered by R, Python, and Google Earth Engine.
- **Story-driven communication** through interactive maps, dashboards, and spatial storytelling that connect science with stakeholders.

### 🔍 Current Themes

- Understanding the role of Conservation Reserve Program lands in wildfire risk in the Great Plains.
- Building geospatial data products that support decision-making for conservation partners and agencies.
- Creating user-friendly tools that allow the public to engage with science.
